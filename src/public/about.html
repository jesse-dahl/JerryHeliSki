About

Jerry was one of the pioneers of guiding in the Chugach Range from Valdez & Cordova to the Copper River. One of the first to discover the "books", "library", "scifi", "flat irons" and other regions! With over 300 first descents in the Chugach Mountains. Jerry's expertise is unsurpassed!

Jerry has been a helicopter skiing avalanche forecaster & lead guide in Alaska since 1992. He has over 28 years experience guiding skiers & snowboarders and forecasting snow stability/instability in Alaska and the Rocky Mountains of U.S. and Canada.

He is a certified instructor for the American Avalanche Association. His experience is coupled with a spotless safety record.
